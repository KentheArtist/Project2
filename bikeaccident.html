Partial Anatomy of a Bike Accident

Tuesday, November 7, 2017. A normal day. Ready to ride.

5 days later, after I woke up at Stroger Hospital's ICU.

My wonderful cousin Diane, who let me recuperate at her house.

Getting better. Hematomas still look like raw meat.

Walking. In pain on my right side, but walking.

Making the most of an unexpected opportunity.

An absolutely vital piece of equipment when I ride now.

My right side has healed, so I'm back to the kind of work I truly love -- doing this kind of illustration in Adobe Illustrator. I've been working in Illustrator since 1988, so I can make it sing, dance and do handstands.

The Rapidograph pens, Paasche airbrushes and ellipse templates have been in the drawer since the mid-90's. That's about the last time a client asked for hand-drawn work.

And this guy? He's my doppelganger and my Facebook and YouTube avatar, Alessandro de' Medici, Duke of Florence, the first head of state of African ancestry in the Western World. Fredrik Reinfeldt, Sweden's prime minister from 2006 to 2014, was the second. Barack was number three.
